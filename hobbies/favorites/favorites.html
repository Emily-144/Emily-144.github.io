Favorites

I enjoy lots of different types of media, and I particularly love seeing the little details and creative efforts in things that show how passionate the creators were. Here, I briefly talk about some of my favorite franchises. I'm always open to new recommendations, too!

Books

One of my favorite book series, that I got hooked on as a kid and am still reading (since the books never end -- there's roughly 100), is Warriors! Warriors is a young adult fiction series about groups of cats who live in the wild.

I tend to enjoy animal-focused stories, so I like reading anything about animals (except those books where the dog dies in the end). Some of my other childhood favorites are Watership Down, the Wings of Fire series (by one of the authors of Warriors), and Wolves of the Beyond.

Movies and TV Shows

I am a huge Star Wars nerd and have watched every movie and TV show that the franchise has produced. Ever since my dad introduced 8-year-old me to the movies, I have been a huge fan. My favorites include The Empire Strikes Back and The Clone Wars TV series. The Thrawn Legends books are on my reading list! I love AT-AT walkers in particular, and I have a growing collection of LEGO AT-AT walkers.

I also love well-crafted animated media. Kids shows like Gravity Falls, Amphibia, and The Owl House do a great job of combining lighthearted jokes with darker themes to make their plots more engaging, and movies like the How to Train Your Dragon trilogy, Puss in Boots: The Last Wish, and Pixar's Up and WALL-E have gorgeous animation in addition to meaningful messages and iconic soundtracks.

Videogames

I tend to enjoy videogames with some sort of adventure and story aspect to them. I grew up playing all sorts of Mario games, and I like to think I'm pretty good at Mario Kart. Of course, I have also played multiple Lego Star Wars games.

Some of my all-time favorites are Stardew Valley, Hollow Knight (and probably Silksong whenever it comes out ?!), Minecraft, Terraria, and Animal Crossing.
